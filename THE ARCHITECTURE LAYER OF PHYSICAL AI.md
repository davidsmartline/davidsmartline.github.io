PAGE 3
Why Current Robot Bodies Are Reaching Their Limits
Every robot designer faces the same trade-offs.
Longer reach means more weight.
Higher payload requires larger motors.
Larger workspaces demand heavier bases.
Complex applications require increasingly complicated mechanical systems.
These compromises are not accidental.
They arise from the architecture itself.
No amount of AI can fully overcome structural limitations.
Changing the architecture changes the limits.

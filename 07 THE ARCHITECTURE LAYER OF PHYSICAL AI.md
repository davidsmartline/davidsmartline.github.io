Every Computing Revolution Begins With A New Architecture.
The history of technology is not a history of better software.
It is a history of new architectures.
Microprocessors enabled personal computing.
The Internet enabled global communication.
Cloud computing enabled AI at scale.
Foundation Models enabled machine intelligence.
The next revolution is Physical AI.
Yet one fundamental layer has barely changed:
The robot body itself.

PAGE 2
The Missing Layer
Today's AI stack looks like this:
Application Layer
↓
AI Models
↓
Operating Systems
↓
Sensors
↓
Motors
↓
Robot Body
Ironically, the lowest layer—the robot body—has seen the least innovation.
Most industrial robots still use serial-link architectures introduced decades ago.
We believe the next leap in Physical AI will come not only from smarter brains, but from fundamentally better bodies.
